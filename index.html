Suggested Learning Path
Step 01
Build the vocabulary
Start with Module 1. Understand Peirce's icon/index/symbol distinction — this is the conceptual lens for everything in the other modules. Focus on why the index (causal connection) is fundamentally different from the symbol (convention).
Step 02
Apply it to TA
Move to Module 2. Go through each pattern card and ask: is this iconic, indexical, or purely conventional? Notice how the manipulation risk scales with how conventional (agreed-upon) the pattern is. Read the self-fulfilling prophecy loop carefully.
Step 03
Build structural ground
Module 3 is the practical framework. Use the HTF Structure Ladder to understand why timeframe matters. Apply the 8-point integrity checklist to any gap you're considering. Return to Modules 1–2 to test your understanding of why the principles hold.
