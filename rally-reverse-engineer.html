Scenario — Daily Chart
Stock gaps up on earnings, forms explosive bullish candle, retests gap, holds. What does this mean?
Apparent conclusion retail reads: "Strong earnings = buy the momentum." Click each backward move to excavate what actually happened.
Hidden Premise — Fully Excavated
The rally was not caused by earnings. Earnings were the public catalyst that gave institutions cover to exit accumulation. Smart money had been building a position in the base for weeks. The gap was their declaration. The explosive candle was the commitment sign. The retest was the last manipulation move — shaking out retail who bought the gap open — before the sustained rally. You didn't need the earnings report. You needed to read the gap structure.
